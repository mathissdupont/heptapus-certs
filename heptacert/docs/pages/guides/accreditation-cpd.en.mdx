# Accreditation & CPD

Automatically log continuing professional development (CPD) hours and produce accreditation/compliance reports. **Accreditation** is an organization module.

<Callout type="info">
If the module isn't visible, enable **Settings → Modules → Accreditation**. At the event level, CPD is controlled by the `cpd_enabled` flag. See [Organization & Modules](/guides/organization-and-modules).
</Callout>

## CPD hour logging

On an event with `cpd_enabled` on, when a **certificate is issued** to a portal-member attendee, the corresponding CPD hours are automatically added to their profile. Attendees thus accumulate their professional development records in one place.

## Accreditation bodies

You track and report CPD hours toward accreditation bodies (such as MYK, SMMM, TMMOB). You can define credits per event/course and produce aggregated reports.

## Compliance tracking

You can track mandatory training/renewal requirements and catch overdue items. With the **`compliance_overdue`** trigger, automatic reminders are sent to those overdue (see [Email & Automation](/guides/email-and-automation)).
