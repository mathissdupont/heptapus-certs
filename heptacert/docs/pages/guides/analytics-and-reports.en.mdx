# Analytics & Reports

Measure your events' performance: registration trend, attendance rate, certificate output, email engagement and per-session breakdowns.

## Event analytics

Each event's **Analytics** view includes:

- **Registration trend** — registrations over time
- **Check-in timeline** — entry intensity
- **Certificate stats** — issued/revoked certificates
- **Email performance** — send, open, click rates
- **Session attendance breakdown** — attendance per session

## Organization-wide analytics

Under **Analytics & Reports → Analytics** you see top-level metrics across all events — total attendees, average attendance per event, period comparisons.

## Reports

From the **Reports** section you can produce PDF/data exports and define scheduled reports. Reports are accessed via API/MCP with the `reports:read` / `analytics:read` scope.

<Callout>
You can ask an AI assistant *"which event had the highest attendance today?"* — the assistant calls the `get_event_analytics` / `get_attendance_summary` tools (see [MCP & AI Agents](/integrations/mcp-agent)).
</Callout>
