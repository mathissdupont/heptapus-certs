# Ticketing

For paid or capacity-limited events, issue attendees a **digital entry ticket**. Ticketing integrates with the registration and check-in flows.

<Callout type="info">
Ticketing is enabled with the `ticketing_enabled` flag in the event **Settings**. Types like concert/show enable it by default.
</Callout>

## How it works

- With ticketing on, each new registration generates a **wallet-compatible, unique QR entry pass**.
- The attendee accesses the ticket from their email or the attendee portal.
- On event day this QR is scanned in the [check-in](/guides/check-in) flow to validate entry.

## Ticket types and capacity

You can define different ticket types (e.g. Standard, VIP, Student) with a quota per type. Capacity can also be managed via option-level capacities on `select` fields in the registration form (see [Registration & Approvals](/guides/registration-and-approvals)).

## Payments

For paid tickets a payment flow kicks in; completed payments are tracked under **Platform → Payments**. When a payment completes, the `payment.completed` webhook event can fire (see [Webhooks](/integrations/webhooks)).

<Callout type="warning">
When tickets + approval are used together: with `requires_approval` on, an attendee isn't counted valid until approved. You can match payment confirmation to this flow.
</Callout>
