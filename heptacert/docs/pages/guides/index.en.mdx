## Typical flow

Most organizers follow this order:

1. **Create the event** and enable the modules its type needs (certificates, check-in, registration).
2. Configure the **registration form**; enable the **approval** flow if needed.
3. On event day, take attendance with **QR check-in**.
4. Once the attendance rule is met, **issue certificates** and make them verifiable.
5. Use **email automation** to automate reminders, certificate delivery and follow-ups.
